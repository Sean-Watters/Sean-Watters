At Coherence, I am working on applications of dependent type theory and the modal μ-calculus in the analysis of Petri nets.

Simultaneously, I am finishing my PhD in the Department of Computer and Information Sciences at the University of Strathclyde, with the [Mathematically Structured Programming Group](https://msp.cis.strath.ac.uk/). 
Prior to that, I did a BSc in Computer Science, also at Strathclyde.

My broad interests are dependently-typed functional programming languages, constructive modal logic, and type theory.
I write a lot of Agda code. 
I like playing with loopy data types.
